Connect Four

What is the app:__ A replica of the classic kids game, the goal is to connect four of your coloured "disks" in a line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) before your opponent does.

Why make the app:__ An exit assignmnet designed to test students on three things:

  • Their ability to interact-with and manipulate the DOM using JavaScript
  • Their comprehension and execution of more advanced concepts (functions, conditionals etc)
  • Their ability to solve a problem - "How do can we mimic gravity and Connect Four's verticality" - using anticipatory decomposition.
