In case you were not aware ...
Mad Libs are stories with words removed and replaced by blank spaces. One player [you, the developer] asks the other player [the user], who haven't seen the story, to fill in the blanks with adjectives, nouns, exclamations, colors, adjectives, and more. After these words are inserted into the blanks, ... the story is read aloud to hilarious results." You are going to write a program that takes a story of your choice, and then allows the user to replace words with (hopefully) far more hillarious words!
